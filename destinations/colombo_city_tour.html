Itinerary

Day 1 – Arrival in Colombo + City Tour

  • Arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport
  • Transfer to Colombo (1 hr)
  • Hotel check-in and relaxation
  • Sunset at Galle Face Green
  • Visit Gangaramaya Temple

Day 2 – Sigiriya + Dambulla

  • Early morning drive to Sigiriya (4-5 hrs)
  • Climb Sigiriya Rock Fortress (UNESCO site)
  • Visit the Dambulla Cave Temple
  • Optional: Village safari or ayurvedic massage
  • Overnight in Sigiriya or Habarana

Day 3 – Kandy via Matale

  • Stop at Matale Spice Garden
  • Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic
  • Scenic walk around Kandy Lake
  • Royal Botanical Garden (Peradeniya)
  • Evening traditional dance show
  • Overnight in Kandy

Day 4 – Nuwara Eliya (Little England)

  • Scenic train ride to Nuwara Eliya
  • Tea plantation visit and factory tour
  • Gregory Lake exploration
  • Visit local strawberry farm
  • Overnight in Nuwara Eliya

Day 5 – Ella or Beach Vibes + Departure

  • Choose your adventure:
  • Option 1: Ella - Nine Arches Bridge & Little Adam's Peak
  • Option 2: Beach Day at Bentota/Negombo
  • Optional water sports activities
  • Evening departure from airport

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit: December to April for west/south coast
  • Transport: Private car with driver recommended
  • Currency: Sri Lankan Rupee (LKR)
  • Must-try: Local food like kottu, hoppers, and dhal
